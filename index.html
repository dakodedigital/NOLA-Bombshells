Who We Are

More Than a
Dance Team.

The NOLA Bombshells promote the development of well-rounded performers who believe in self-expression, confidence, and community. Our dancers participate in outreach, traditional performances, and parade culture rooted in New Orleans tradition.

Our symbol — a bold set of red lips — represents the smile we share with every crowd, every charity partner, and every community we serve. We don't just perform. We give back.
